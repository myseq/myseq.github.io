*Color 13432, formally known as Federal Standard 595C 13432 or National School Bus Glossy Yellow (AMS-STD 13432)*.
{{< /lead >}}

This s a distinct orangish-yellow color specifically formulated for North American school buses. 

It is designed to be highly visible, utilizing a specific wavelength to catch the eye.
It is recognized as a vibrant, glossy yellow-orange. 

## Color 13432

This color was standardized in **1939** to ensure visibility and safety.
It is chosen because it is a **yellow-orange hue** that remains highly visible in low light and provide maximum readability for black lettering.

Although the color looks *orange* to some people, it is technically a *yellow-orange mix* optimized for vilibility. 

Key details:

 - Hex Code: #F5A500 (RGB Values: 245, 165, 0)
 - Common Use: Primarily used for school buses to ensure safety, adopted in 1939 for its high visibility.
 - Application: Commonly used in spray paints, touch-up bottles, and acrylics for modeling or industrial applications. 

> It is often referred to as "Chrome Yellow" (FS 13432) in hobbyist paint sets.
