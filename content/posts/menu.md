*If you are not on the table, then you are in the* ***menu***.
{{< /lead >}}

This is a *political and business maxim*.
It means that if you are not involved in decision-making, you will be affected by those decisions.
It is often associated with political power, business strategy and negotiation.

It highlights a zero-sum power dynamic where you either influence rules and resources,
or become a subject of them.
