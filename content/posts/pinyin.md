{{< lead >}}
*Setup Pinyin with traditional Chinese character input on Windows 11.*
{{< /lead >}}

**Windows 11** ***Pinyin input*** gives us to type Chinese character using US keyboard.
However, it allows us to type simplify Chinese character only by default.

To install **Pinyin** input:

 - Goto **Language & region**
 - Click **Add a language** and select **Chinese (Simplified, Mainland China)**
 - Just select **Language pack**, and the rest are optional
 - Click **Install**

Now, once we switch (win + space)  the keyboard to Chinese Pinyin, we can start typing *simplify Chinese character* by default.

## Traditional Chinese Character

In order to be able to type *traditional Chinese character*:

 - Goto **Language & region** again
 - Click on the 3-dot at the right of **Chinese (Simplified, Mainland China)**
 - Scroll down to **Keyboards** section
 - Click on the 3-dot at the right of **Microsoft Pinyin**, select **Keyboard options**
 - Click **General**
 - Scroll to **Choose a character set**, and select **Traditional Chinese**

Now, once we switch (win + space)  the keyboard to Chinese Pinyin again, we can start typing ***traditional Chinese character***.
